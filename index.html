Welcome to Almac & Sons

Your trusted family-run garage offering MOTs, servicing, and repairs.

With over 30 years of experience, we pride ourselves on quality workmanship and excellent customer service. Whether you need a quick MOT or a full service, our skilled mechanics are here to help keep your vehicle running smoothly.

All vehicles will be subject to a safety inspection prior to any work being carried out. Customers will be advised of any defects or necessary repairs before any work begins.

Any parts removed will be held pending customer instructions regarding disposal. Customers will be given a copy of the safety inspection sheet detailing any work required in order to make informed decisions about their vehicle.

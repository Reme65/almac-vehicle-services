About Us

Almac & Sons Vehicle Services is a family-run business proudly serving the local community. Our team of qualified mechanics specialises in MOT testing, vehicle servicing, diagnostics, and general repairs for all makes and models.

Customer safety and satisfaction are at the heart of everything we do. We ensure every vehicle leaving our garage is roadworthy and compliant with legal MOT standards.
